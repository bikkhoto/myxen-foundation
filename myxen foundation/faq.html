General Questions

What is MyxenPay? +

MyxenPay is a comprehensive crypto payments platform built on Solana blockchain that enables:

  • Global QR code payments for merchants
  • Real-time streaming payroll for businesses
  • Student rewards and cashback programs
  • VISA virtual card integration
  • Instant settlements with minimal fees
How is MyxenPay different from traditional payment processors? +

Traditional Processors (Stripe, PayPal):

  • 2-3% transaction fees
  • 2-5 day settlement periods
  • Geographical restrictions
  • Bank account requirements
  • Chargeback risks

MyxenPay:

  • 0.5% transaction fees (0.02% for basic transfers)
  • Instant settlements
  • Global accessibility
  • No bank account needed
  • No chargebacks
What blockchain does MyxenPay use? +

MyxenPay is built entirely on Solana blockchain due to its:

  • High transaction speed (65,000 TPS)
  • Low transaction costs ($0.00025 per transaction)
  • Proven security and reliability
  • Strong ecosystem and developer support
  • Environmental efficiency (Proof of History consensus)
Is MyxenPay available in my country? +

MyxenPay is available globally in all countries where cryptocurrency usage is permitted. Since we're a non-custodial platform:

  • No geographical restrictions for basic payments
  • Works anywhere with internet access
  • No currency conversion fees
  • 24/7 availability worldwide

Note: Some advanced features may have regional restrictions based on local regulations.

Merchant Services

How do I start accepting crypto payments as a merchant? +

Getting started takes less than 5 minutes:

  1. Visit MyxenPay Merchant page
  2. Enter your Solana wallet address
  3. Generate your unique QR code
  4. Display the QR code in your store or online
  5. Start accepting payments instantly

No registration, no KYC, no waiting period.

What are the fees for merchants? +

MyxenPay offers the lowest fees in the industry:

  • Basic Crypto Payments: 0.5% per transaction
  • $MYXEN Token Payments: 0.25% (50% discount)
  • Streaming Payroll: 0.5% flat fee
  • VISA Virtual Card: 1% + network fees
  • No hidden fees, no monthly charges

Compare to traditional processors: Stripe (2.9% + $0.30), PayPal (3.49% + fixed fee)
