Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Real Revenue Burns

Why burn tokens?

Token burns reduce the total supply, increasing scarcity and potentially increasing value for all holders. It's a sustainable deflationary mechanism.

How is the burn amount calculated?

30% of all platform revenue is used for buybacks. The exact number of tokens burned depends on market price at time of purchase.

When do burns happen?

Burns will be executed monthly after platform launch. All transactions will be verifiable on-chain for complete transparency.

Can I verify the burns?

Yes! All burn transactions will be publicly verifiable on Solana blockchain explorers like Solscan and announced on our social channels.
